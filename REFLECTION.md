# Cookie Clicker Reflection

## What helped you understand DOM interaction best?
What helped me understand the DOM interaction best was the direct connection between JavaScript and HTML using ids. Once that connection is declared in JavaScript, it's simple to make changes.

## How did you choose your milestone messages?
I based my milestone messages on the samples and found ways to better focus them on my theme, based on the twist and color changing features I added. 

## What challenge or bug surprised you?
I had to find a way to cycle through the colors without adding an extra variable to keep count. I found I could do this mathmatically using the count of the clicker, and the modulo to repeatedly cycle through the items in the array.

## What personal twist did you add?
I themed my clicker off candy, and after every five clicks the clicker background would also cycle through the colors of the rainbow. 

## What real-world app uses this kind of interaction?
Real-world social media apps often use a similar form of interaction to count likes on a post. Every like adds to the total count, and after each like the new total is displayed on the page.
